11/16/2001

A slow Friday. Unemployment starts to drag after a few weeks. I've been reading a ton - all the 87th Precinct novels by Ed McBain and anything by Dan Simmons that I haven't read already. If you have never read anything by Dan Simmons, drop what you are doing right now and buy something by him. He's a great author and almost everything he writes is superb (I think the third and fourth books in the Hyperion series are the exception). The high-powered sports cars he puts in many of his later novels are merely icing on the cake. How about a sports bike in the next one, Danny boy? :-)
