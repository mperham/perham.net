The Day’s Activities

Sunday, September 19th, 2004

One of the things Becky and I did yesterday was buy some new clothes for myself. I haven’t bought any new clothes in probably a year (most guys count on Christmas for their yearly influx of new clothes) and my family thinks I wear Large, when in fact I wear Medium. This meant that all my Christmas clothes this year made me look like a huge fat guy (when in fact I am just a moderately sized portly guy).

So I budgeted $200 to get a few new things. We walked through Norstrom’s first whereupon I learned that a decent looking shirt costs $100. After that, the prices at the Gap seemed decent (only $50 for a shirt!). In the end, I spent $250 and got 3 shirts, 2 pants and a pullover sweater.

In the evening, we went to a party hosted by Becky’s old high school friend, Rob. He’s a karkoake nut so we had to sing. Yeah. So I belted out Duran Duran’s View to a Kill and Motley Crue’s Girls Girls Girls. Since I knew nobody there, I’m hoping any political future I might have is safe.
