Back from the Track

I took yesterday off work to head up to Motorsport Ranch in Cresson for another track day. It was a pretty good day: Ben Spies, Suzuki Factory AMA Superbike racer, was there to get his CMRA racing license. I got my knee down for the first time three different times. And I had a ride there and back so I didn’t have to deal with the terrible drive home. My legs of course are killing me but that’s just part of the cost.
