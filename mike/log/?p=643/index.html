Cutting the Cable

I cancelled my TWC cable. When I think about life, I realize I’ll never say the phrase “I wish I had watched more television”. The racing season is over and I’ve got a new Tivo HD to handle the over the air broadcast channels so I’m not completely TV-free, but I am free of the “500 channels and nothing’s on” syndrome.
