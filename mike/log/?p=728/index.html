Advanced Micro Devices

I bought some AMD yesterday. I find that in the last 10 years, Intel and AMD’s stock fluctuates based on the quality of their current chip. AMD’s latest chip sucks and they overpaid for ATI so their stock has plummeted in the last year. Nevertheless I think the market has overcorrected; I bought them at the “price of the beast” so we’ll see if they can recover and make me a little green.
