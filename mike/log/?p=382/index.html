America at War

One of the things that makes me most angry about the Bush Administration’s actions over the last few years is their continual insistence that America is at war and will continue to be until we defeat the terrorists. Until then they need to be able to spy on Americans without oversight, compile “no-fly” lists of “terrorist” names with no accountability and send agents to visit students doing book reports.

What a load of crap. Did you know that Egypt’s “president” has led the country for the last 25 years in a “state of emergency”?

Wars are fought against other governments. America is not at war. Average people, no matter how well equipped, who kill other people are criminals. Nations who harbor those people are corrupt states. When civil liberties are restricted, it is expected that this is due to an ongoing and urgent need. Being on a war footing or in a state of emergency for years or even decades against a criminal element thousands of miles away makes no sense and disgraces the original intent of this country as a nation based on freedom for all.
