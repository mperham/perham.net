More Books

Management Challenges for the 21st Century

A fascinating book that takes demographic and economic trends and portends how these changes will affect business and society. One of the things I liked best about the book is the sense of history - Drucker does not just cover the last decade, he draws from experiences over the last century! If you read this blog, you need to read this book.

Inversions

A Greythistle recommendation. I did not find this book terribly compelling. Storywise, it’s not bad but I expected the two narratives to intertwine somehow and in the end, did not get the resolution I was expecting (maybe I’m just dumb but I didn’t see how the tales were related).
