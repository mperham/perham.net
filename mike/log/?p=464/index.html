Now We’re Cooking

I decided to start cooking a little on my own. I bought a cookbook the other night and made Peanut Noodles tonight. It took me about 30 minutes to make, 3 minutes to eat and 10 minutes to clean up. At least I have enough leftovers for 2 more dinners. I don’t expect to be a gourmet but it would be nice to know enough not to look foolish in the kitchen.

It was edible but rather bland. Too much basalmic vinegar and not enough chile.
