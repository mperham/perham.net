ARGH! DVD formats are killing me!

No, not literally. I bought a spindle of 50 DVD+R discs to burn because I read on the web that the +R format was the most compatible (89% of all current DVD players). I got my DVD burner on Monday and the drive came with a DVD+R disc so I burnt some video to it and tried it in my DVD player. Nothing. Then I do a quick web search for details on my DVD player and there is much gnashing of teeth when I find that no Panasonic DVD players support the + formats. In fact, most of the DVD players available on bestbuy.com mention support for DVD-R but very few mention support for DVD+R. So it looks like I'm going to try to exchange my +R spindle for the -R format and hopefully that will work better.
