Ponderings on Monitors

Since I need to buy a new flat panel monitor, I've been researching them and try to decide which one I want. I've come to the conclusion that I'm going to try to wait for the next generation Dell 20-22" display. Dell traditionally is not a leader in hardware, that is usually reserved for Apple and a few others (e.g. Shuttle creating the whole XPC industry two yrs ago). However Dell is excellent at taking cool hardware and making it a commodity. Given that, the time seems right for Dell to introduce a large widescreen display. Apple introduced them a few years ago so it seems natural to expect Dell's next refresh of its LCD panel line to include one. To get an Apple display would cost me at least $1200-$1500. I would expect the equivalent Dell to be under $1000. Now let's wait and see if I'm right.
