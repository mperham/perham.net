Wherein Dad pimps his Ride

I’m going to order a Mac mini next week once they are guaranteed to ship with Tiger. Trent Reznor of NIN fame just released his latest single in GarageBand format for hobbyists to remix. Should be a fun way to kill a few weekends in the coming months.
