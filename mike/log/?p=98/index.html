Random Stuff

Wrist is back to normal now. I cancelled my appt to see the doctor again. I don’t need to pay him $50 to learn I’m doing fine.

I’ve been getting estimates to replace the windows in my house. I’ve learned more about window technology in the last 3 days than I ever knew before. Now I know how badly my current windows suck (40 years old, aluminum, thin single pane glass) - they basically are transparent to heat. Looks like good new windows are about $400+ each installed and I have seven to install. Ouch.

Got to meet and chat with Eric Bostrom at the Ducati Austin open house the other day. I can’t wait to see how he does this year in AMA Superbike - he’s my pick to win; the new 999 is supposed to be great although I think he will have some serious competion from his brother on the new Honda CBR1000RR.
