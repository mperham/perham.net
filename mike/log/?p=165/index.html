Innovation vs. Big Media

Here’s the latest saga in the ongoing struggle between small companies trying to innovate and big media companies. Also note the system as described at the end of the article would be next to useless for most people anyhow. It’s extremely complex to set up and most people do not have the bandwidth to easily upload video files to the Internet. I know at home I might get 20-30KB/sec on a good day. Let’s say I get 33KB/sec, that’s a megabyte every 30 seconds. An hour of motorcycle racing is about 1GB. So it would take me about 500 minutes or about 8.5 hrs to upload it to a single person. Really useful.
