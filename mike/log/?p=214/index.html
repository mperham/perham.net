Latest Books

I read a bunch of books during the vacation. Some quickie reviews:

Angels and Demons by Dan Brown - the prequel to “The Da Vinci Code”. This guy is obviously the latest author in the tradition of Tom Clancy, John Grisham and Michael Crichton. The book is fast paced and is written in a style easily translatable to the big screen. Very entertaining and a great, no brain required read.

Fortress of Solitude by Jonathen Lethem - my latest Lethem novel and it definitely did not disappoint. This book is fantastic. It’s an unbelievably rich portrait of the life and times of two boys growing up in 70s Brooklyn.

Hard Freeze by Dan Simmons - the sequel to “Hard Case”, Simmons’s foray into the hard boiled detective genre. Simmons is a unique author in that he has tried out different fiction genres over the course of his career, usually successfully IMO. Similar to Demons and Angels in that there’s a high explosion to thought ratio here but not quite as good. I still enjoyed the book.

Superluminal by Vonda McIntyre - the only sci-fi book of the bunch and a letdown. The first 70 pages or so are simply boring and then the book becomes a mediocre “man uses unexplained super powers to change the world” plot line.
