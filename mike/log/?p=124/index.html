MotoGP 2004

This article foreshadows a very exciting upcoming year of MotoGP racing. It certainly looks like Honda will have their work cut out for them holding off Rossi on his new Yamaha along with the four Ducati riders. Even Suzuki appears to be somewhat competitive this year… and it’s about time.

The incredible top speeds have me puzzled though - barely anyone broke 200 mph last year at Catalunya whereas basically everyone did this year. I have to believe there was a decent tailwind this year (or a headwind last year) because it’s simply too much to chalk up to engine advances.

At any rate, 4/17 just might be the most exciting day this year!
