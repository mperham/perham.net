Hacked!

My NSLU2 is now 0wned by yours truly. Rod Whitny discovered how to hack the root account of the NSLU2 with nothing more than tar and a text editor. Once that was done, I grabbed dropbear courtesy of Brian Lantz and installed it. I can now ssh into my NSLU2.

Brian’s dropbear binary does not support scp or sftp so it’s usefulness is limited. The built-in busybox binary is also pretty limited so I will need to upgrade that. None the less, this little box is turning out to be quite useful.
