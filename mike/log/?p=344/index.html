Gallery upgrade failed

Gallery 2 was recently released. I tried upgrading to it last night only to find that my web host, pair networks, limits PHP scripts to 8MB of RAM whereas G2 requires 16MB minimum to work. This means that I can’t use G2 unless I can think of a workaround.

On a related note, my gallery is back in its old location.
