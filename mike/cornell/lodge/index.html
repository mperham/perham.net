Cayuga Lodge

630 Stewart Ave. (607) 273-0101

Welcome to the Cayuga Lodge Home Page. Cayuga Lodge is a student cooperative providing affordable housing and a friendly atmosphere for Cornell students. The history of the Lodge goes back some 60 years to the opening in 1933. Here you can meet the current members, read a little about the history of the Lodge and find out about current openings.

Lodge Amenities

  • Grads, Undergrads, Women, Men - everyone welcome
  • Student owned - No landlord
  • Close to Cornell - across from Noyes Center on Stewart Avenue
  • Social activities - Parties, Movies, etc.
  • Nice rooms at low cost ---- Doubles - $215-260 Singles - $290-340
  • 10-month leases with greatly reduced summer rent
  • Parking available for approx. $10/month
  • Free laundry
  • TV room with a RCA satellite connection - over 100 channels

We hope this web page has interested you enough to come check us out. If you are interested in living here, please give our current Vice-President, Rebecca Baker, a call at 273-0101 and leave a message.
